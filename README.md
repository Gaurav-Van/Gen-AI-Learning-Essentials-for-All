# (Gen) AI Learning Essentials for All

## Purpose

(Gen) AI Learning Essentials for All helps everyone get comfortable with the core ideas and terminology behind AI and GenAI through Easy to Grasp, Illustrative, Visualized and Animated explainers. This is an introductory, non‑assessed awareness resource, a door‑opener that removes jargon, builds intuition, and primes learners formal Upskilling programs. It’s designed for all, especially for folks entering AI with no prior background, non‑technical colleagues who want the gist without code, and technical practitioners seeking a quick refresher on the building blocks. 

## Who it’s for
Everyone - no prior background needed. Non‑technical colleagues who want the gist without code. Technical practitioners seeking a quick refresher on the building blocks

## 3 Steps Towards (Gen) AI
Explore the _**Fundamentals of AI**_, including an overview of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning, and understand their differences. Delve into the _**Building Blocks of Generative AI**_, supervised and unsupervised learning, and various ML models. Discover neural networks like CNNs, and key concepts in NLP such as tokenization and word embeddings. Learn about advanced models like RNNs, LSTMs, transformers, and GANs. Finally, understand the _**World After Large Language Models (LLMs)**_, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and the process of fine-tuning LLMs, practical courses from Hugging Face & Microsoft, along with Resources for Microsoft's Agent Framework to build Agentic Systems
